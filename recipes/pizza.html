PIZZA RECIPE

Description

If you follow these simple steps you will enjoy an amazing home-made pizza at your home! It is healthier to prepare your Pizza at home and use fresh products instead of buying the ultra-processedpizzas that you see in the supermarket

Ingredients

  • 200 gr of weat
  • 200 ml of water
  • 6 gr of yeast
  • 200 gr of pomodoro
  • Fresh basilic
  • Burrata
  • Salt
  • Honey

Steps

first you need to pour 200 ml of water inside a bowl with 200 gr of weat, some salt and 6 gr of yeast.

Then you need to knead the dought until it reaches a firm consistency. Don't forget to add salt and a spoon of honey for a better result. Afterwards the dought needs to rest for about 30 minutes, and then you separate the dought in two slices and lave them rest for an hour

Finally you can take the dought and form the base of the pizza. Pre-heat the oven at 250ÂºC for about 30 minutes. It is better to use a slab stone for a better result. You put then the pomodoro on top of the pizza and baked it for about 7 minutes

When you take out the pizza, put the rest of the ingredients on top: the burrata and the fresh basilic... and enjoy!
