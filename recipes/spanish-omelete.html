SPANIS OMELETE RECIPE

Description

If you follow these simple steps you will enjoy an amazing home-made spanish omelete at your home! It seems easy but this is a difficult dish!

You need to carefully follow the steps provided under

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • 3 medium size potatoes/li>
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. peel the potatoes and cut them very thin. That is the first important trick. Make thin and small slices
  2. put enough oil to fry the potatoes, and put them when the oil is hot enough. You don't want to fry the potatoes fast, it is a slow process . Mince the garlic and put it in the pan when the potatoes are nearly done
  3. beat the 5 eggs in a bowl. When the potatoes are done retire them and put them inside the bowl. Leave the potatoes in the bowl for 10 minutes
  4. While the potatoes are in the bowl resting, retire the excess of oil and leave enough oil to make the final omelete... and enjoy!
  5. Put all the ingredients on the pan and with a wood spoon brake constantly the surface in order to make a solid cooked base. Then you need to flip the omelet using a plate or the cover of the pan
  6. You can cook the omelete as long as you want, but it tastes better when it is more liquid
