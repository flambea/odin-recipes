VEGETABLE SOUP

By following these simple stemps you can prepare a delicious vegetable soup, very easy and healthy

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of water
  • 200 gr of green beans
  • 1 potato
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 zucchini
  • 5 spoons of olive oil
  • 5 gr of Salt
  • 3 leeks
  • 100 gr of peas

Steps

  1. Choop and put all the vegetables in the pot, cover it with the water and put the salt and the oil
  2. The mix needs to boil during 16 minutes
  3. Then you need to blend the mix with the blender and that's all!
